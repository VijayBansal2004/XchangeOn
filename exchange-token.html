XchangeOn Token

Get Distribution Details

Returns Details Of Next Token (crypto-dividends) Profit Distribution 

GET https://api.xchangeOn/api/v2/XchangeOn/tokenholderdistribution

Provides details on when the next token distribution is, and a countdown.

It is important to note that the token distribution should not be considered complete until user's have received their crypto dividends. If a user transfers XchangeOn out of their wallet prior, they may not receive payouts.

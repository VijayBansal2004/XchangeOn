Q1–Q2 2023
XchangeOn Batch 3 Complete
  • Our XchangeOn options will be expanded by onboarding Gate.io, BitMart, and HTX, giving users access to additional trading platforms.
Blockchain Batch 2 Complete
  • Ripple, Cardano, and Polygon Network integration will enable support for a broader range of blockchain platforms as well as assets.
Combined Order Book Complete
  • Make it possible to view combined order books and trades from several exchanges. To help users make better trading decisions, show them the cumulative sizes at important resistance levels.
Q3–Q4 2023
XchangeOn Batch 4 Complete
  • Increase XchangeOn accessibility and provide additional trading opportunities by integrating OKCoin, ProBit, Bitstamp, and OKX.
Blockchain Batch 3 Complete
  • Expanding blockchain compatibility through support for Bitcoin SegWit, Litecoin SegWit, Arbitrum, and Avalanche C-Chain.
Self-Hosting Node Deployment Complete
  • Hosting our own nodes to reduce transaction fees and increase speed. Initially, Ethereum, Bitcoin, Litecoin, Solana, Polygon, and XRP will be hosted on nodes.
Q1–Q2 2024
Fiat On-Ramp Complete
  • Collaborate with fiat on-ramp aggregator Rampnalyis. To simplify user transactions, provide direct coverage for all major fiat currencies.
DeFi Exchanges Complete
  • Include Radium, PancakeSwap, and Uniswap in the XchangeOn. Expand trading options by introducing the first group of decentralized providers.
Decentralized Aggregator Integrations Complete
  • Integrate decentralized XchangeOn aggregators, such as 1 inch and Jupiter. Offer users a multitude of new trading pairs and sources of liquidity.
Q3–Q4 2024
Instant Settlement Complete
  • Allow users to trade instantly on all centralized and decentralized sources. Ensure full collateralization against funds for enhanced security.
Enhanced UI (Beta Launch) In Progress
  • Deploy UI V2 with a sleeker interface and improved user experience. Introduce arbitrage tools and advanced charting features.
Improved API Coverage Planned
  • Integrate wallet methods into our API for seamless transactions. Deploy a WebSocket API for high-frequency market data feeds catering to advanced users.
Q1–Q2 2025
XchangeOn Batch 5 Planned
  • Boost trading opportunities by adding BTSE, Crypto.com, CEX, and Kraken to our list of supported exchanges.
Multichain Token Launch Planned
  • Set up presale and private sales for Tokens.
  • Launch contracts for multichain tokens on the Ethereum, Arbitrum, and Solana networks. Introduce a stake-to-earn system that can reduce trading fees by up to 80%.
Futures Integrations Planned
  • Introduce an alpha version of futures trading for the general public and testers. Give users access to advanced account features that let them manage their XchangeOn accounts independently of their assets.
Q3–Q4 2025
AI Integration Planned
  • Build an trained model using advanced learning models and GPT APIs.
  • Provide unmatched backtesting and chart generation capabilities.
Centralized XchangeOn Token Listing Planned
  • Introduce Token on a connected centralized XchangeOn.
  • To encourage participation, start token staking with live fee reductions.
Marketing and Social Outreach Planned
  • Launch the first round of the airdrop for early backers, HFTs, and high-volume users.
  • Announce strategic alliances to increase market presence.
Q1–Q2 2026
Institutional Platform V2 Planned
  • Improve the prime brokerage user interface for institutional clients.
  • Add an arbitrage screener for advanced trading strategies.
  • Establish an institutional market-making assistant to improve the management of liquidity.
Decentralized $ Bridge Planned
  • Execute minting on target chains while causing matching burns on the source chain.
  • Improve accessibility and utility by offering complete multichain support.
Options Integrations Planned
  • Add Deribit and PowerTrade as official option trading partners.
  • Provide advanced options accounts that enable sophisticated trading by separating funding rates, expiries, strategies, and collateral.
